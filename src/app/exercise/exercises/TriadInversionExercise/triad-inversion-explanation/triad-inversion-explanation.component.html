In this exercise you will hear a triad in one of the three possible inversions, and you will need to identify what inversion it is.

An inversion is the order of which the chord notes are played (from lowest to highest).

Here are the possible inversions:

  • Root position (Example: C E G). Also called: 5th position
  • First inversion (Example: E G C). Also called: Octave position
  • Second inversion (Example: G C E). Also called: 3rd position
If you're just starting out, check out the "Arpeggiate Speed" field in the settings.
